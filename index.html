Field Trip is an open source documentary about an unconventional place: Tempelhof Field - an airstrip in the heart the Berlin metropolis. Field Trip tells the story of what for some is a 300 ha free urban paradise, for others a place of forced labour and for yet others a safe haven. By using the technique of open hypervideo and intense on-site and online community development, Field Trip aims at becoming a living documentary.
